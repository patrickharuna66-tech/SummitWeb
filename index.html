FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Summit Web.

How fast will my website be ready?
Your website will be ready in 7–14 days depending on complexity. We prioritise speed without compromising quality and keep you updated on WhatsApp throughout the entire process.
Are the pricing packages one-time payments?
Yes — all listed packages are one-time payments, no subscriptions, no hidden charges, no surprises. What you see is exactly what you pay. You can also add services anytime via the Custom Plan page.
What does "Full AI Setup" include?
We integrate AI chatbots, automated lead follow-up sequences, AI-powered content tools and WhatsApp Business automation — saving you hours every single week while generating leads around the clock.
What kind of support do you offer?
Priority support via WhatsApp, phone and email. Our average response time is under 1 minute during business hours (Mon–Sat, 9am–7pm WAT). Critical issues have a dedicated response line.
Can I build a completely custom package?
Absolutely! Our Custom Plan builder lets you pick exactly the services you need and see a transparent price total before you commit — no pressure, no hidden costs.
Do you work with businesses outside Jos?
Yes, we work with businesses across all 36 states in Nigeria as well as internationally. All our services are delivered fully online, with communication via WhatsApp, email and video calls.
